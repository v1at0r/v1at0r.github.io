Mudit Aggarwal

Math Masters student at UBC Vancouver

Hey there! I am Mudit, a Master’s student at the Department of Mathematics, UBC, fortunate to be advised by Dr. Andrew Rechnitzer. I completed my undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Engineering from IIIT Delhi.

When I am not studying math, watching anime, or failing academically, I like to trek and play tennis. I also like running, playing chess, and trying to boulder. I spend way too much time on Reddit and Spotify as well.

At IIIT Delhi, I have had the pleasure of working with Dr. Samrith Ram, Dr. Manuj Mukherjee, and Dr. Anuradha Sharma.

At the University of Victoria, I have had the pleasure of working with Dr. Aaron Gulliver and Dr. Sajin Koroth.

Currently, I am dabbling in Analytic Combinatorics, Representation Theory, Information Theory, and Coding Theory. I am also exploring other fields like Functional Analysis.

I am a firm believer in \(\tau\) supremacy in the \(\pi\) vs \(\tau\) debate.
